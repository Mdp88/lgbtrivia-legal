How to delete your LGBTrivia data

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Leer en español

LGBTrivia has no accounts and no servers of our own. Everything the app stores lives only on your device, so deleting it is immediate: there's no external copy for us to remove on your behalf.

From inside the app

Open LGBTrivia, go to the Settings tab, and you'll find separate buttons to:

  • Reset your questionnaire history
  • Reset the identities unlocked in your album
  • Reset your trivia progress and score
  • Reset the initial disclaimer acceptance

Each button asks for confirmation before deleting, and takes effect immediately.

By uninstalling the app

If you'd rather delete everything at once, uninstalling LGBTrivia from your device is enough. Android automatically removes all of the app's local storage at that point.

What this doesn't cover

Ads in the app run through Google AdMob, which may collect a device advertising identifier independently of what LGBTrivia itself stores. That data is managed by Google, not us, and you can reset or limit its use from Google Ads Settings or from your device's own privacy settings.
