LGBTrivia Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

LGBTrivia is built so your answers never leave your device. We don't run our own servers and there are no user accounts: we can't see, sell, or share what you answer in the questionnaire because that information never reaches us in the first place.

What information is stored, and where

  • On your device only: your questionnaire answers, identities unlocked in your album, trivia score and progress, and your language preference. These are stored using AsyncStorage, the app's local storage tool, and are removed if you uninstall the app or use the reset options inside Settings.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any directly identifying information, other than what Google independently processes as described below.

Ads (Google AdMob)

LGBTrivia shows ads through Google AdMob so the app can be offered for free. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers and other technical information to serve ads, including personalized ads if you gave consent. This processing is carried out by Google, not by us directly. You can review how Google handles that data in its privacy policy and adjust your personalized ad preferences at Google Ads Settings.

In-app purchases (LGBTrivia Plus)

The one-time "Plus" purchase is processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment information; we only receive confirmation that the purchase succeeded, so we can unlock the corresponding content on your device.

Children

LGBTrivia is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your choices

You can clear all of your progress at any time from Settings inside the app, or remove all data by uninstalling the app. Since we don't store anything on our own servers, there is no separate account or external copy to request or delete.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. We recommend checking back from time to time.
